# Pathos
## Emotional Appeal

---

## Definition

**Pathos** is an appeal to emotion. It engages the audience's feelings—fear, hope, anger, joy, sympathy—to persuade through emotional connection rather than logic alone.

---

## Purpose

- Connect emotionally with audience
- Make abstract issues personal
- Motivate action through feeling
- Create empathy and understanding
- Make arguments memorable

---

## When to Use

- **Persuasive writing**: Motivate readers to care/act
- **Storytelling**: Create emotional investment
- **Speeches**: Move audiences to action
- **Marketing**: Connect products to feelings
- **Advocacy**: Humanize statistics and issues

---

## Techniques

### Tell Stories
Personal narratives create emotional connection:
- Individual cases over statistics
- Vivid, specific details
- Human faces on abstract issues

### Use Vivid Language
Emotional words and imagery:
- Sensory details
- Powerful verbs and adjectives
- Metaphors that evoke# Pathos
