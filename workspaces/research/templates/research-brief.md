## Risks and Mitigation

### Potential Risks

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation Strategy |
|------|------------|--------|-------------------|
| Insufficient sources | High | High | Broaden search terms, expand timeframe |
| Low-quality sources | Medium | High | Strengthen evaluation criteria |
| Incomplete information | Medium | Medium | Acknowledge gaps in findings |
| Time constraints | Low | High | Prioritize high-impact sources |

### Contingency Plans

If search yields insufficient sources:
- Expand search terms and synonyms
- Extend timeframe if possible
- Broaden inclusion criteria
- Consult with experts for recommendations

If sources are low quality:
- Re-evaluate search strategy
- Focus on highest-quality available sources
- Clearly document limitations
- Adjust research question if needed
